ABOUT SWIFT

Swift is the idea-generation competition that needs only one thing, your brilliant idea!

What do you need to join?

A concept. No long proposals, no research, no hassle. Let your concept drive your competition away, and make your way to creating your app and a cash prize of Php 25,000!

Process

  • THINK

    Conceptualize a mobile application that YOU think is useful, reliable and beneficial to users, but be as creative and out-of-the-box as you must.

    • All college and HS students residing in the Philippines are eligible to join the competition. Participants may join individually or by pair.
    • You may choose to fill up the online form below with your idea or a printed form, available in the UP Circle of Entrepreneurs Tambayan (Virata School of Business, UP Diliman). There is no maximum number of entries for each individual or pair. Deadline of submission of forms is on October 18,2014.
    • The top 100 will be chosen on the following criteria
      • Creativity - 50%
      • Feasibility - 30%
      • Relevance - 20%

  • Build

    Start making your dreams into reality by building up your mobile application. How it looks, how it helps, how it works.

    • The Top 100 entries will be forwarded to Ideaspace for further screening, Ideaspace has the right to call in participants to undergo their organization’s training for a chance to execute the mobile app and have it available to the market! And you don’t even need to pay to make your idea real: Ideaspace will finance and help you build it. Exciting, isn’t it?

  • EXCEL

    Be part of our Swift event, presenting your idea not just to us, but also to a panel of judges.

    • The panel of judges will be selecting the top 10 entries based on the same criteria. Top 100 entries and The Top 10 entries that are qualified for the Panel Round will be released on October 19, 2014.
    • Entries qualified for the Panel Round will present their idea in front of a panel and audience in not more than 3 minutes.
    • Judges will select the overall winner based on the following criteria:
      • Market Potential
      • Creativity
      • Feasibility
      • Presentation
      • Relevance

  • The best will receive P25000 cash!
